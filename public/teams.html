Teams

Viderent Partners teaches teams how to test innovative ideas quickly and efficiently.  Using instructor-guided “customer discovery” methods (and design thinking), teams talk to customers and stakeholders, determining the need, market and potential of an idea.  

Viderent’s Rapid Experimental Innovation (REXIN) model, developed based on guiding more than 600 teams, expands and improves upon gold-standard NSF I-Corps models and standard Lean methods.
